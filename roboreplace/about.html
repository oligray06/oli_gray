About

RoboReplace was founded in 2029 and serves as a fully autonomous replacement service. Our droids are packed with the latest is artificial intelligence and real time full-body deep faking in order to make the most convincing replica of yourself possible.

A chosen droid will be able to complete almost all tasks that you normally would throughout your day, using our top-end cloud servers, we are able to learn all about what tasks you perform and school, then replicate them to an indiscernible degree.

Users are most impressed by the remote monitoring feature which allows for the client to log into the droid from a mobile phone or computer and use the cameras and microphones built in to monitor the droid’s “performance”.

After a droid’s day is done, it will return to a syncing station, wipe all information associated with the client including any mannerisms and personal features that is has learnt and becomes a blank slate ready for the next user.
